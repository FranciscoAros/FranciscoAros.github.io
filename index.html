Dr. Francisco I. Aros

I am an astrophysicist developing mathematical models to better understand the velocity distribution of galaxies and star clusters and building a bridge between simulations and observations. This allows us to not only robustly extract the underlying invisible mass contributions from dark matter in the case of dwarf galaxies or intermediate-mass black holes in the case of globular clusters but also to better understand their dynamical state and evolution.
